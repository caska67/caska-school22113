Aqoon Bile School is a place where students grow into future leaders. We emphasize holistic development through academics, sports, and moral values. With top-tier faculty and facilities, we ensure each student is prepared to succeed in life.

Our vision is to create a nurturing environment where every learner can achieve academic excellence, develop life skills, and become a responsible citizen of the world.

About Us

Founded in 2008, Aqoon Bile School has been a pillar of quality education in the region. Our curriculum balances theory and practice, with extracurriculars ranging from robotics to literature clubs.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students to become critical thinkers, lifelong learners, and responsible global citizens through innovative teaching methods and a nurturing environment.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Somalia that transforms lives through excellence in teaching, research, and community engagement, producing graduates who make a positive impact on society.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Respect, Innovation, and Community. These core values guide everything we do at Aqoon Bile School and shape the character of our students.

Admissions

We welcome students from diverse backgrounds. Our admission process is simple, inclusive, and based on merit and potential.

  • Collect application form online or from the admin office.
  • Submit required documents (birth certificate, previous reports, etc.).
  • Take an entrance assessment (for Grades 2 and above).
  • Attend parent-student interview session.
  • Receive admission decision within 7 working days.
  • Complete registration and payment process.
